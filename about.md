# About This Database

Welcome to the **Personalities Database** – a comprehensive collection of personality profiles for characters from Japanese media, including anime, manga, light novels, and video games.

## Our Mission

We aim to provide detailed, well‑researched personality analyses that explore the psychological depth and cultural nuances of fictional characters. Each profile is generated using a combination of data from Fandom wikis and insights into Japanese storytelling traditions, ensuring authenticity and richness.

## How It Works

The database is built with Jekyll and automatically updated via GitHub Actions. The character data (names and personality descriptions) is stored in plain text files, and a Python script generates individual pages for each character. This approach allows for easy expansion and maintenance.

## Why We Created This

Fictional characters often reflect complex human emotions and cultural values. By cataloguing their personalities, we hope to:

- Help writers and creators understand character archetypes.
- Provide fans with deeper insights into their favorite characters.
- Showcase the diversity of character design in Japanese media.

## Contact

If you have any questions, suggestions, or would like to contribute, feel free to open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/characterswiki/Personalities).
